WHAT'S PANCAKE

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

PANCAKE is THE original ping pong social club. Our flagship is located in New York City, where our story began almost ten years ago. (Ask us about Naked Ping Pong. Don’t worry, it’s not weird.) We now have eight (and counting) venues throughout the US and Canada. All of our venues center around ping pong and offer a minimum of twelve courts, in addition to a full-service restaurant and craft cocktail bar. Plus, we have the coolest and friendliest staff in the biz.
